Dr. Amit Salkar

  • MBBS, MD, DNB (CARDIOLOGY) Intervention Cardiologist

Dr. Amit Salkar is an experienced cardiologist who specialises in a wide range of cardiac interventions (including angiography, angioplasty, pacemaker implantation, and the management of heart failure). He has successfully completed over 10,000 angiographies and over 5,000 angioplasty procedures. To him, the most fascinating things are the most advanced and intricate methods. Intuitive vascular ultrasound-guided angioplasty is a complex technique, and he has effectively taught a session on it. Complex coronary angiography, chronic total occlusions, percutaneous aortic valve replacement, heart disease, mitral valve replacement, and coronary heart disease are Dr. Amit Salkar's primary areas of clinical focus as an interventional cardiologist.
