Dr. Amit Salkar

  • MBBS, MD, DNB (CARDIOLOGY) Intervention Cardiologist

A versatile cardiologist with extensive training in cardiac interventions, Dr. Amit Salkar (Angiography, Angioplasty, pacemaker, Heart Failure management) has performed more than 10,000 Angiographies and more than 5,000 Angioplasties successfully. He is particularly interested in complex and cutting-edge procedures. He has executed a challenging IVUS-guided angioplasty workshop successfully.
Dr. Amit Salkar's main clinical focus areas as an interventional cardiologist are complex coronary angiography, chronic total occlusions, percutaneous aortic valve replacement, cardiovascular disease, mitral valve replacement, and coronary heart disease.
