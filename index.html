Haematology and Heart Care Clinic

Our Haemacare center's goal is to provide complete, high-quality medical care in a friendly, effective environment. We offer personalised treatment, such as shorter wait times and expert care for all kinds of cases, even the most complicated ones.

Cardiology

Cardiology is the study and treatment of the cardiovascular system, which includes the heart, arteries, and veins. Our cardiology department caters to all heart- related tests to detect all the diseases related to the heart. We have been adapting to all of the new cardiology tests in order to help patients understand all of their heart problems.

Haematology

The study of blood and blood problems is known as hematology. Our Hematologists are highly trained healthcare professionals specializing in blood and blood component diseases. These consist of bone marrow and blood cells. Anemia, infections, hemophilia, blood-clotting problems, and leukemia can all be diagnosed with hematological tests.

General Health Care

The Haem and Heart Care Clinic's mission is to assist its patients in leading healthy lives by providing them with an early diagnosis of disease and providing assistance with a variety of preventative treatments. Our high-quality medical services cover a wide variety of treatment modalities.

Services we provide

Our specialists have vast expertise and knowledge in their respective domains. The clinic is set up to offer the highest level of treatment while empathetically with a whole multidisciplinary team of hemato-oncologists, lab hemato-pathologists, infectious disease specialists, cardiologists, surgeons, and skilled laboratory technicians.
